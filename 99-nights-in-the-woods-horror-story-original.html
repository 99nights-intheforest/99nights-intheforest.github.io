About 99 Nights In The Woods: Horror Story Original

99 Nights In The Woods: Horror Story Original is a chilling Roblox survival horror game where you must endure 99 nights in a haunted forest, facing the Man-Eating Deer, aka Skinwalker, and other eerie creatures. Launched in 2025, it tests your wits with intense nighttime survival challenges.

How to Play 99 Nights In The Woods: Horror Story Original

Survive 99 nights with these steps: Use mouse or finger to gather logs during the day, fueling the campfire to ward off nighttime terrors. Monitor cameras to spot the Skinwalker or other creatures. When the Owl appears in the left corner, click/swipe to shoo it away, preventing it from extinguishing your fire. If the Man-Eating Deer nears the flames, run to the house (click the house icon) and hide silently until it leaves. Nights run from 12 AM to 6 AM, with increasing threats. Use earned coins for upgrades like stronger flashlights. For tips, visit survival guide.

99 Nights In The Woods: Horror Story Original delivers heart-pounding horror, testing your survival skills against the Skinwalker. Keep the fire lit, hide smart, and team up to conquer 99 nights. Play now on Roblox at https://99nights-intheforest.github.io!
